Topic: How can we support dsylexia in the workplace?

Because dyslexia affects learning, it is often considered a disability and thus protected by the ADA. Employers are not allowed to discriminate based on this person's dyslexia or learning disability.

Comments:

Give verbal as well as written instructions (this also applies to using voicemail rather than, or as well as, written memos)



Topic: How can we support a collegue with visual impairment in the workplace?

The most important rule is to ask a person who is visually impaired what their preferred mode of communication is and let them know what format you can provide information in. Some people may prefer email communication as the person may use text to speech software or the person may use magnification.

Comments:

Provide information in alternative formats
